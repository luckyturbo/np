Professional Steering Motor Services

Steering motors have become standard in modern vehicles, providing efficient and responsive steering assistance. At Lucky Turbo Repairing Center, we specialize in comprehensive steering motor repair and maintenance services to ensure your electric power steering system operates reliably and efficiently.

Our Steering Motor Services Include:

  • Rotating Group Services: Comprehensive inspection and repair of motor rotating components
  • Bearing Replacement: Precision replacement of worn bearings to reduce noise and vibration
  • Brush and Commutator Service: Inspection and replacement of brushes and commutator assembly
  • Complete Overhaul: Full disassembly, inspection, and rebuilding of steering motor assemblies

Common Signs of Steering Motor Issues:

  • Intermittent or complete loss of power steering assistance
  • Steering wheel vibration or irregular feedback
  • Unusual whining or grinding noises from steering column
  • Erratic steering assistance or varying steering effort
  • Steering wheel not returning to center properly

Don't let steering motor issues compromise your driving safety and comfort. Contact us today for professional steering motor repair services.
